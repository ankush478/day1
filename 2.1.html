Dhaba style masala maggi at home

Ingredients

  1. Maggi – 1 packet
  2. Oil – 1 tbsp
  3. Butter – 1 tsp (optional but yum!)
  4. Onion – 1 small (finely chopped)
  5. Tomato – 1 medium (finely chopped)
  6. Green chilli – 1 (optional)
  7. Capsicum – 2–3 tbsp (optional but adds great taste)
  8. Ginger-garlic – ½ tsp (optional)
  9. Water – 1 cup
  10. Maggi masala – 1 packet
  11. Coriander leaves – for garnish
  12. Cheese – optional
  13. Extra spices (for that dhaba taste):
    1. Red chilli powder – ½ tsp
    2. Turmeric – ¼ tsp
    3. Coriander powder – ½ tsp
    4. Garam masala – ¼ tsp
    5. Chat masala – ¼ tsp

    Steps to make perfect maggi

    1. Heat oil + butter

      2. In a pan, heat oil and a little butter for flavour.

    2. Saute the veggies

      • Add onion → sauté until golden.
      • Add capsicum, chillies → cook 30 seconds.
      • Add tomatoes → cook until soft.
      • Add ginger-garlic if using.

    3. Add the spices

      4. Add:

      • Maggi masala
      • Red chilli
      • Turmeric
      • Coriander powder
      • Garam masala
      • Chat masala

      Mix them and cook for 10–15 seconds so they release aroma.

    4. Pour water

      5. Add about 1 cup water (slightly less for thicker Maggi). Let it boil.

    5. Add Maggi

      6. Break the noodles into 2 pieces and add. Cook on medium flame 2 minutes, stir occasionally.

    6. Make it creamy (optional)

      7. Add a bit more butter or grated cheese for extra richness.

    7. Garnish and serve

      8. Top with coriander, chilli flakes, or cheese.
